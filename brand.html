Logo Usage Guidelines

  • Minimum size: 100px width (digital) or 1 inch (print)
  • Maintain clear space equal to the height of the logo "c"
  • Do not distort, stretch, or modify the logo
  • Do not change logo colors
  • Do not add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
  • Use on appropriate backgrounds with high contrast
  • Scale proportionally only

Usage Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to maintain brand consistency and ensure proper representation of contraco's visual identity.

Logo Usage

  • Use official logo files only
  • Maintain minimum size requirements
  • Preserve clear space around logo
  • Use on appropriate backgrounds (high contrast)
  • Scale proportionally only
  • Do not distort or stretch
  • Do not change logo colors
  • Do not add effects (shadows, outlines, gradients)
  • Do not place on busy backgrounds
  • Do not recreate or modify logo elements

Color Usage

  • Use exact HEX/RGB values provided
  • Primary Red for brand emphasis and CTAs
  • Dark Grey for primary text
  • Maintain sufficient contrast for accessibility
  • Do not approximate colors
  • Do not use unapproved color variations
  • Do not place low-contrast color combinations

Typography Usage

  • Use specified fonts for each medium
  • Maintain hierarchy (headlines vs body)
  • Ensure readability and legibility
  • Follow weight guidelines for emphasis
  • Do not substitute with similar fonts
  • Do not use decorative or script fonts
  • Do not over-style text (excessive bold/italic)
